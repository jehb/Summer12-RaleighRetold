Overview

Help us tell Raleigh's untold stories with this interactive, multimedia applicatiton available on Google Android devices. Remember Raleigh through your eyes and tell its history from your perspective. Then, share and socialize with other users to really capture Raleigh's true essence and enjoy seeing the city through other's eyes.

Features

  • Tell your stories by uploading photo, video, and audio files. (Only Video available on version 1)
  • Search nearby stories using gps functionality.
  • Share stories you tell through Facebook.


(Default "you are here" location is Brooks Hall on NCSU campus when GPS is disabled.)
